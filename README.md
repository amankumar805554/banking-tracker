## üìñ About The Project

**Daily Focus** isn't just a to-do list; it's a **Shinobi's tool for discipline**. Designed for students and developers who want to gamify their productivity, this app combines the "Pomodoro" technique with a "Mission Book" system inspired by the Naruto universe.

It features a robust **Dual-Save Engine**, allowing it to work perfectly offline (Local Storage) or sync to the cloud (Firebase) when you log in.

---

## ‚öîÔ∏è Key Features

### üî• The "Sharingan" Experience
* **Dynamic Theming:** Switch between **Light**, **Dark**, and **Sharingan (Red/Black)** modes.
* **Immersive UI:** The interface changes colors, browser headers, and chart aesthetics to match your active Dojutsu.

### üìú Bingo Book (Mission System)
* **Ranked Missions:** * **D-Rank:** Daily Tasks.
    * **B-Rank:** Weekly Goals.
    * **S-Rank:** Monthly Milestones.
* **Gamified Completion:** Slash through tasks with custom CSS animations.

### üìä Chakra Flow Analytics
* **Real-time Visualization:** Your "Chakra" (Time Spent) is visualized in a Doughnut chart.
* **Deep Stats:** Track your focus history over 7, 30, and 90 days.
* **Focus Timer:** A built-in countdown timer with Lap functionality.
